Projects

Racquetball App

For my Senior project, I collaborated with three students to develop custom software for automating racquetball tournament management, including scheduling and scorekeeping. The project comprised four parts: a backend Django server, a web scraper for tournament data, an Android app for referees, and a scheduling app for managing tournaments. I was primarily responsible for the scheduling app, which enabled users to log in, schedule matches, and edit tournament data. Additionally, I managed client relations, conducted weekly meetings, and ensured we met our goals and delivered a product our client loved.

Cowboy Simulator

For our final game design project, I collaborated with three other students to develop a third-person game in Unity. The game’s premise may be quirky—you must collect all the spaghetti ingredients to save the town—but it features impressive gameplay elements. We implemented functional movement for various actions, reload animations, and adaptive AI to enhance the gaming experience. Our game also included a redesigned UI that allowed players to interact with NPCs and navigate through a pause and boot menu. Through this project, I gained proficiency in C# and Unity, while also honing essential soft skills like collaboration and communication.
